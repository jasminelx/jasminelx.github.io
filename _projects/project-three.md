Plastic is the second short play I wrote for the Kings Players theatre society, performed in February of 2026. This was my first attempt at writing drama, rather than comedy. It questions what agency means in a aesthetics-obsessed society that insists that all choices can be empowering. 

This play was written as part of the King's Players' Scratch Night, a collection of short student plays.
